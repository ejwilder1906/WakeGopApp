About District 4

District Chair: Matthew Arnold

North Carolina’s Fourth Congressional district spans seven counties, linking the largest cities in each. The Fourth is a diverse community including all of the major universities in the Triangle, and virtually every significant attraction that North Carolina has to offer. The Republican party remains focused on attracting like-minded voters as well as candidates committed to representing the best interests of the district

About District 13

District Chair: Zan Bunn

The 13th Congressional District covers nine counties Edgecombe, Franklin, Durham, Granville, Nash, Vance, Wake, Wayne, and Wilson. The most important function of the 13th Congressional District is to help elect Republicans into office at the local, state, and national level. The 13th Congressional District is devoted to energizing and cultivating a grassroots organization at all levels that is critical in serving as an all-inclusive support system for our nine local county Republican parties.
